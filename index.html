What we do

We are a company aiming to offer affordable aerial photography for any range of applications. Aerial photography is a great way to make your event or property stand out. The ability to show friends, fans and customers the top level down will add a real touch of class and professionalism.

What can we do for you?

Events
Whether a wedding, a concert, sports or any event, a top level camera will provide a unique and memorable perspective. Outdoor events in particular will benefit from the breathtaking angle and sweeping panoramas available.
Real Estate
Aerial photography allows you to show the entire property in a single shot, as well as give flying tours of interesting features, giving you a fresh angle and a unique appeal to all properties, large and small.
Agricultural
A fly over by a drone is faster and cheaper than by a traditional plane, and allows for a fine grained inspection of crop areas, allowing for increased yields, time savings and crop health inspections. Aerial documentation can also be a useful component of crop insurance claims.
